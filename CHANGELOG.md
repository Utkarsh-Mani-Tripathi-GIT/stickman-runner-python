## [1.0.0] — 2026-03-31

### 🎮 Initial Release

#### Added
- **Stickman character** with 4-frame run animation, jump, duck/slide, and death sprites
- **Cactus obstacles** — 4 variants with colored flowers, scaling difficulty
- **Fighter jet enemies** — 4 flight patterns: straight, dive, sine-wave, and fast
- **Missile system** — Jets fire missiles with exhaust trails and dynamic aim
- **Near-miss detection** — Dodge counter tracks close calls with visual sparks
- **Formation attacks** — Multiple jets attack simultaneously at high danger levels
- **Particle system** — Dust, sparks, explosions, exhaust trails
- **Screen shake** — Dynamic shake on landing, missile fire, collisions, and death
- **Day/night cycle** — Smooth gradient transitions every 600 points
- **8-bit sound engine** — Mario-inspired synthesized SFX:
  - Jump, land, duck sounds
  - Coin and milestone jingles
  - Death melody
  - Jet flyby, missile launch, near-miss warning
  - Speed-up notification, explosion, siren, dodge confirmation
- **HUD elements:**
  - Score counter with flash effects at milestones
  - High score tracking (per session)
  - Speed indicator with color-coded warnings
  - Danger level meter
  - Dodge counter
  - Pulsing red warning border during enemy encounters
- **Title screen** with animated stickman and flying jet
- **Credits screen** with author info and LinkedIn link
- **Environment:** Procedural clouds, stars, moon, and textured ground
